User Data Deletion Instructions

According to Facebook Platform rules, we provide a way for users to request the deletion of their data.

How to Request Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your data from AKGS Empire, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to undercover00yt@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include your username or connected wallet address.
  3. We will process your request and remove all your data from our servers within 30 days.

Alternatively, you can remove our app access via your Facebook/Instagram settings:

  • Go to your Facebook Settings & Privacy > Settings.
  • Look for "Apps and Websites" and you will see all of the apps and websites you linked with your Facebook.
  • Search for "AKGS Empire Social".
  • Scroll and click "Remove".
